- YATRII -

Yatrii, known by his real name Sagar Saud, commenced his musical journey in 2017, dedicating himself to mastering the art of crafting captivating songs. Originally from Sudurpashchim, Achham, and currently based in Kathmandu, Yatrii harbors significant aspirations in the music industry and establishing himself as a Nepali Melodic Rapper, Singer, Songwriter, and Producer. Influenced by the legendary Yama Buddha, Yatrii initially delved into the complexities of lyrical rap, a foundation he later enhanced by integrating melodic elements into his distinctive style. Inspired by globally acclaimed artists such as Russ, Juice WRLD, iann dior, Toosii, Rod Wave, Post Malone, XXXTENTACION and Fresco Trey, Yatrii has cultivated a unique fusion of lyrical prowess and melodic sensibilities. This distinctive blend has positioned him in the ever-evolving landscape of new school Nepali rap. Take a journey into Yatrii's extraordinary musical world, where his words come alive, melodies dance, and dreams take flight
